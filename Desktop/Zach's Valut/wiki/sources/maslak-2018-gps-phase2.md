## Key Data Already Extracted (from prior session)

### Patient Population
- n=22 enrolled; n=14 with immunologic data available
- AML patients — disease setting (CR1 vs CR2 mix) to be confirmed from PDF

### Immune Response Rates
- **CD8+ response (HLA-A\*02:01+ patients):** 6/7 tested = **86%**
  - Cross-reactivity to native WT1-A confirmed in all 5 ELISPOT-tested patients
- **CD4+ response:** 4/9 tested = **44%**
  - None of the 4 CD4+ responders relapsed

### Figure 5 — DFS/OS by Immune Response (KEY)
- **DFS (Panel A):**
  - Responders (n=9): median not reached, plateau **~55–60%** at 65+ months; p=0.11
  - Non-responders (n=5): median 15.6 months, tail ~20%
- **OS (Panel B):**
  - Responders: median not reached, plateau **~65–70%** at 65+ months; p=0.08
  - Non-responders: median 35.8 months, tail ~20–25%
