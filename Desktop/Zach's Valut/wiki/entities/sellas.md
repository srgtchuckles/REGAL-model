## Overview
SELLAS Life Sciences Group, Inc. is the late-stage clinical biopharmaceutical company sponsoring the REGAL trial and developing galinpepimut-S (GPS). Understanding their corporate situation (cash runway, partnerships, trial management) is relevant context for interpreting communications and timelines.

## Key Facts

- **Ticker:** SLS (NASDAQ Capital Market) — formerly listed as SELS
- **Address:** 7 Times Square, Suite 2503, New York, NY 10036
- **Structure:** Delaware corporation, non-accelerated/smaller reporting company
- **CEO:** Angelos M. Stergiou, MD, ScD h.c.
- **CFO:** John T. Burns, CPA

### Financial Position (as of May 12, 2026)
- **Cash:** $107.1M (March 31, 2026) + $7.5M post-Q1 warrant exercises = **~$114.6M**
- **Accumulated deficit:** $283.4M
- **Shares outstanding:** 186,032,574 (as of May 11, 2026)
- **Stock price reference:** $4.23/share on March 31, 2026
- **Runway:** "At least 12 months" from May 2026 filing date — no going concern risk
- **ATM facility:** Up to $150M with TD Cowen (2026 ATM, established March 2026, unused as of filing)
- **Q1 2026 burn:** $8.4M net loss; $8.8M operating cash outflow

### Product Candidates
1. **GPS (galinpepimut-S):** Lead candidate. Licensed from MSK. Phase 3 REGAL trial ongoing (see [[entities/regal-trial]]). Orphan Drug + Fast Track designations in AML, MPM, MM. RPD designation for pediatric AML (Oct 2024). BLA prep underway.
2. **SLS009 (tambiciclib):** CDK9 inhibitor licensed from GenFleet (ex-Greater China). Phase 2 in r/r AML met all primary endpoints (July 2025). Advancing to first-line randomized 80-patient Phase 2 trial (enrollment started Q1 2026). IMPACT-AML European partnership (Jan 2026).

### Partnerships & Licensing
- **3D Medicines Agreement (Dec 2020):** GPS/GPS+ rights for Greater China. $10.5M received to date; $191.5M potential future milestones outstanding.
  - **Arbitration:** Commenced Dec 2023 at HKIAC. Evidence hearing held January 2026. Outcome pending. Claims: ~$13M in milestone payments + commercially reasonable best efforts breach.
- **GenFleet Agreement (March 2022):** SLS009 rights worldwide ex-Greater China. $10M upfront paid; up to $140M development/sales milestones.

## BLA Preparation Signal
Q1 2026 R&D spend reveals aggressive GPS BLA preparation ahead of REGAL final analysis:
- Manufacturing costs: +$1.3M YoY (explicitly for BLA prep)
- Clinical/regulatory consulting: +$0.4M YoY (explicitly for BLA prep)
- External GPS trial expenses: +$0.2M YoY

This pre-commercialization spend pattern is consistent with management anticipating a positive REGAL outcome, but carries obvious corporate incentive bias.
