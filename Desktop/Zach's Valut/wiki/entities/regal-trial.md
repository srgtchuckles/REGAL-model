## Overview
REGAL is a Phase 3 randomized controlled trial evaluating galinpepimut-S (GPS) versus best available therapy (BAT) in patients with acute myeloid leukemia (AML) in second complete remission (CR2). It is the pivotal trial for GPS's potential FDA approval.
