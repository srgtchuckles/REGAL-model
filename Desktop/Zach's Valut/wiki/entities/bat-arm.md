## Overview
The BAT arm is the comparator in the REGAL trial. "Best available therapy" is an investigator-selected control, meaning each patient in this arm receives whatever therapy the clinical team judges most appropriate — which could include hypomethylating agents, low-dose chemotherapy, supportive care, or allogeneic SCT.

## Why BAT Composition Matters
The heterogeneity of BAT is a critical analytical variable. If many BAT patients received allo-SCT (a potentially curative intervention), the control arm performance may be stronger than historical benchmarks, making it harder for GPS to show a significant OS benefit.
